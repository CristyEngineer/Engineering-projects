# ⚡ Low‑Voltage Distribution Board Mounting Plate
Design & Engineering of Mounting Configurations for Compact Substations

This project focuses on the engineering and optimization of mounting solutions for a low‑voltage distribution board mounting plate used in compact substations and walk‑in substations. The work was carried out for a supplier in the underground energy‑infrastructure sector. All company details have been anonymized. Using AutoCAD and Autodesk Inventor, multiple mounting configurations were designed, modelled and evaluated. The project aimed to improve not only efficiency, but also productivity, safety, capacity and cost‑effectiveness within the substation design process.

# 🎯 Project Summary
The objective of this project was to:
- Develop several mounting concepts for the LV distribution board plate
- Model a complete compact substation layout in 2D and 3D
- Compare solutions based on technical requirements and stakeholder needs
- Improve installation safety, accessibility and maintainability
- Reduce material usage and simplify assembly steps
- Support higher production capacity through standardization

The final design provides a clear, manufacturable and installation‑friendly mounting solution suitable for different substation configurations.

# 🧩 Methods & Approach
The project followed a structured engineering and design‑methodology approach aligned with the learning outcomes of the module:
- Problem exploration and requirement gathering
- Stakeholder interviews
- Comparison of multiple feasible solutions
- 2D drafting and 3D modelling of mounting configurations
- Evaluation of alternatives based on safety, cost, capacity, productivity and technical constraints
- Selection and refinement of the preferred mounting method

# 📊 Key Outcomes
- A validated mounting solution with improved safety and accessibility
- Reduced assembly time and lower risk of installation errors
- Better use of available space inside compact substations
- Lower material and production costs through simplified geometry
- A scalable design approach supporting future product variations
- Clear engineering documentation suitable for production and field teams
